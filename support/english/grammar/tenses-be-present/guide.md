# be動詞の現在形

be動詞は「〜です」「〜にいる」を表す動詞です。

## 📌 主語によるbe動詞の変化

I `am` a player.

The game `is` fun.

Games `are` fun.

- I → `am`
- he / she / it → `is`
- you / we / they → `are`

## 📊 活用パターン

| 主語 | be動詞 | 例 |
|------|--------|------|
| I | `am` | I `am` a player. |
| he / she / it | `is` | The game `is` fun. |
| you / we / they | `are` | Games `are` fun. |

## 📝 例

| 主語 | 文 |
|------|------|
| I | I am a player. |
| He | He (`is`) a gamer. |
| She | She (`is`) a player. |
| They | They (`are`) gamers. |
| We | We (`are`) players. |
| It | It (`is`) fun. |
