# be動詞の未来形

現在形:
I `am` a player.

## 📚 未来形

I `will be` a player.

- `will be` + 形容詞/名詞で未来を表す
- 主語に関係なく `will be` の形

## 📊 活用パターン

| 時制 | be動詞 | 例 |
|------|--------|------|
| 現在形 | am/is/are | I `am` a player. |
| 過去形 | was/were | I `was` a player. |
| 未来形 | will be | I `will be` a player. |

## 📝 例

| 現在形 | 未来形 |
|--------|--------|
| I am a player. | I (will be) a player. |
| The game is fun. | The game (will be) fun. |
| I am good at games. | I (will be) good at games. |
| I am at the game center. | I (will be) at the game center. |
| I am happy to play. | I (will be) happy to play. |
