# 関係副詞 - 解説

2文:
This is the place. I was born `there`.

1文（関係副詞）:
This is the place `where` I was born.

- 場所・時・理由を表す先行詞に使う

## 関係副詞一覧

| 関係副詞 | 先行詞 | 例 |
|----------|--------|------|
| `where` | 場所 | the place `where` I was born |
| `when` | 時 | the day `when` I met you |
| `why` | 理由 | the reason `why` I was late |
