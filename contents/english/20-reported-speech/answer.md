# 間接話法ドリル - 解答

## 穴埋め問題

### 時制の一致

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| He said, "I play games." | He said that he (played) games. |
| He said, "I am playing games." | He said that he (was playing) games. |
| He said, "I played games." | He said that he (had played) games. |
| He said, "I will play games." | He said that he (would play) games. |
| He said, "I can play games." | He said that he (could play) games. |
| He said, "I have played games." | He said that he (had played) games. |

### 疑問文の間接話法

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| He asked, "Do you play games?" | He asked (if / whether) I played games. |
| He asked, "What do you play?" | He asked (what) I played. |
| He asked, "Where do you play?" | He asked (where) I played. |

### 命令文の間接話法

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| He said, "Play games." | He told me (to play) games. |
| He said, "Don't play games." | He told me (not to play) games. |

### 指示語・時の表現の変化

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| "I will come tomorrow." | He said he would come (the next day). |
| "I came here yesterday." | He said he had gone (there) the day before. |
| "I am busy now." | He said he was busy (then). |
| "I like this book." | He said he liked (that) book. |

## 英作文

| 日本語 | 英語 |
|--------|------|
| 彼はゲームをすると言った。 | He said that he played games. |
| 彼はゲームをしたと言った。 | He said that he had played games. |
| 彼は私にゲームをするように言った。 | He told me to play games. |
| 彼は私にゲームをするかと聞いた。 | He asked me if I played games. |
| 彼は私に何をするかと聞いた。 | He asked me what I did. |
