# 疑問文の間接話法ドリル

## 穴埋め問題

( ) に正しい語を入れましょう。

基本文: He asked, "Do you play games?"
→ He asked `if` I played games.

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| He asked, "Do you play games?" | He asked (　　　　　　) I played games. |
| He asked, "What do you play?" | He asked (　　　　　　) I played. |
| He asked, "Where do you play?" | He asked (　　　　　　) I played. |
