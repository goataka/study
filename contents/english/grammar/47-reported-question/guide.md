# 疑問文の間接話法 - 解説

直接話法:
He asked, "Do you play games?"

## 間接話法

He asked if I `played` games.

- Yes/No疑問文: `if` / `whether` + 平叙文の語順
- 疑問詞疑問文: 疑問詞 + 平叙文の語順

## パターン

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| "Do you play games?" | He asked `if` I played games. |
| "What do you play?" | He asked `what` I played. |
| "Where do you live?" | He asked `where` I lived. |

## 解答

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| He asked, "Do you play games?" | He asked (if / whether) I played games. |
| He asked, "What do you play?" | He asked (what) I played. |
| He asked, "Where do you play?" | He asked (where) I played. |

## 英作文解答

| 日本語 | 英語 |
|--------|------|
| 彼は私にゲームをするかと聞いた。 | He asked if I played games. |
| 彼は私に何のゲームをしたか聞いた。 | He asked what games I played. |

## 応用問題

直接話法の疑問文を間接話法に変えましょう。

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| She asked, "Do you like cooking?" | She asked 　　　　　　　　　　　　 I liked cooking. |
| He asked, "Where do you live?" | He asked 　　　　　　　　　　　　 I lived. |
| She asked, "What time does the train leave?" | She asked 　　　　　　　　　　　　 the train left. |
| He asked, "Did you finish your homework?" | He asked 　　　　　　　　　　　　 I had finished my homework. |
| She asked, "Why are you laughing?" | She asked 　　　　　　　　　　　　 I was laughing. |

## 応用解答

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| She asked, "Do you like cooking?" | She asked `if` I liked cooking. |
| He asked, "Where do you live?" | He asked `where` I lived. |
| She asked, "What time does the train leave?" | She asked `what time` the train left. |
| He asked, "Did you finish your homework?" | He asked `if` I had finished my homework. |
| She asked, "Why are you laughing?" | She asked `why` I was laughing. |
