# 指示語の変化 - 解説

直接話法:
"I will play games `tomorrow`."

## 間接話法

He said he would play games `the next day`.

- 間接話法では指示語・時の表現が変化する

## 変化一覧

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| `now` | `then` |
| `today` | `that day` |
| `tomorrow` | `the next day` |
| `yesterday` | `the day before` |
| `here` | `there` |
| `this` | `that` |

## 練習問題

( ) に正しい語を入れましょう。

直接話法の指示語が間接話法で変化します。

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| "I will come tomorrow." | He said he would come (　　　　　　). |
| "I came here yesterday." | He said he had come (　　　　　　) the day before. |
| "I am busy now." | He said he was busy (　　　　　　). |
| "I like this book." | He said he liked (　　　　　　) book. |
| "I will do it today." | He said he would do it (　　　　　　). |

## 解答

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| "I will come tomorrow." | He said he would come (the next day). |
| "I came here yesterday." | He said he had come (there) the day before. |
| "I am busy now." | He said he was busy (then). |
| "I like this book." | He said he liked (that) book. |
| "I will do it today." | He said he would do it (that day). |

## 英作文

日本語を英語にしましょう。

| 日本語 | 英語 |
|--------|------|
| 彼は明日来ると言った。（間接話法で） | 　　　　　　　　　　　　 |
| 彼は今忙しいと言った。（間接話法で） | 　　　　　　　　　　　　 |

## 英作文解答

| 日本語 | 英語 |
|--------|------|
| 彼は明日来ると言った。（間接話法で） | He said he would come the next day. |
| 彼は今忙しいと言った。（間接話法で） | He said he was busy then. |
