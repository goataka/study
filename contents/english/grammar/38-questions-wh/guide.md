# 疑問詞 - 解説

疑問文:
`Do` you play games?

## 疑問詞

`What` do you play?

- 疑問詞を文頭に置く
- 疑問詞 + 疑問文の語順

## 疑問詞の一覧

| 疑問詞 | 意味 | 例 |
|--------|------|------|
| `what` | 何 | `What` do you play? |
| `who` | 誰 | `Who` plays games? |
| `when` | いつ | `When` do you play games? |
| `where` | どこ | `Where` do you play games? |
| `why` | なぜ | `Why` do you play games? |
| `how` | どのように | `How` do you play this game? |
| `which` | どちら | `Which` game do you play? |
| `whose` | 誰の | `Whose` game is this? |

## 解答

| 疑問詞 | 疑問文 |
|--------|--------|
| 何を？ | (What) do I play? |
| どこで？ | (Where) do I play games? |
| いつ？ | (When) do I play games? |
| なぜ？ | (Why) do I play games? |
| どのように？ | (How) do I play games? |

## 英作文解答

| 日本語 | 英語 |
|--------|------|
| 私は何をしますか？ | What do I do? |
| 私はどこでゲームをしますか？ | Where do I play games? |
| 誰がゲームをしますか？ | Who plays games? |

## 応用解答

| He was born in 2010. | `When` was he born? |
| They go to school by bus. | `How` do they go to school? |
