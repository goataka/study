# 所有格の関係代名詞 - 解説

2つの文:
I have a friend. His father is a doctor.

## 所有格の関係代名詞

I have a friend `whose` father is a doctor.

- 所有格の関係代名詞は `whose`（人・物共通）

## パターン

| 先行詞 | 関係代名詞 | 例 |
|--------|-----------|------|
| 人 | `whose` | a friend `whose` father is a doctor |
| 物 | `whose` | a house `whose` roof is red |
