# 所有格の関係代名詞 - 解説

2つの文:
I know a boy. His father plays games.

## 所有格の関係代名詞

I know a boy `whose` father plays games.

- 所有格の関係代名詞は `whose`（人・物共通）

## パターン

| 先行詞 | 関係代名詞 | 例 |
|--------|-----------|------|
| 人 | `whose` | a boy `whose` father plays games |
| 物 | `whose` | a game `whose` rules are simple |
