# 所有格の関係代名詞 - 解説

2つの文:
I know a boy. His father plays games.

## 所有格の関係代名詞

I know a boy `whose` father plays games.

- 所有格の関係代名詞は `whose`（人・物共通）

## パターン

| 先行詞 | 関係代名詞 | 例 |
|--------|-----------|------|
| 人 | `whose` | a boy `whose` father plays games |
| 物 | `whose` | a game `whose` rules are simple |

## 解答

| 文 |
|------|
| I have a friend (whose) father is a doctor. |
| I have a book (whose) cover is red. |
| I have a friend (whose) mother is a teacher. |

## 英作文解答

| 日本語 | 英語 |
|--------|------|
| お父さんが医者の友達がいる。 | I have a friend whose father is a doctor. |
| 表紙が赤い本を持っている。 | I have a book whose cover is red. |

## 応用問題

2つの文を whose を使って1つにしましょう。

| 2つの文 | 1つの文 |
|---------|---------|
| I have a friend. Her mother is a nurse. | I have a friend 　　　　　　　　　　　　 mother is a nurse. |
| He knows a boy. His dog is very large. | He knows a boy 　　　　　　　　　　　　 dog is very large. |
| She bought a house. Its garden is beautiful. | She bought a house 　　　　　　　　　　　　 garden is beautiful. |
| I met a girl. Her sister is my classmate. | I met a girl 　　　　　　　　　　　　 sister is my classmate. |
| We visited a museum. Its collection is famous. | We visited a museum 　　　　　　　　　　　　 collection is famous. |

## 応用解答

| 2つの文 | 1つの文 |
|---------|---------|
| I have a friend. Her mother is a nurse. | I have a friend `whose` mother is a nurse. |
| He knows a boy. His dog is very large. | He knows a boy `whose` dog is very large. |
| She bought a house. Its garden is beautiful. | She bought a house `whose` garden is beautiful. |
| I met a girl. Her sister is my classmate. | I met a girl `whose` sister is my classmate. |
| We visited a museum. Its collection is famous. | We visited a museum `whose` collection is famous. |
