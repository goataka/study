# unless - 解説

if を使った条件文:
If I `don't` have time, I won't play games.

## unless

`Unless` I have time, I won't play games.

- `unless` = `if ... not`（～しない限り）
- unless の後の動詞は肯定形

## パターン

| 表現 | 例 |
|------|------|
| if not | If I `don't` have time, I won't play games. |
| unless | `Unless` I have time, I won't play games. |
