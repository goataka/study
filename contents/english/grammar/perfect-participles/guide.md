# 過去分詞の変化

過去分詞は完了形や受動態で使います。

## 変化のルール

規則動詞は過去形と同じ（-ed）:
play → `played`

不規則動詞は個別に暗記:
go → went → `gone`

## 不規則動詞の活用パターン

| 原形 | 過去形 | 過去分詞 | 意味 |
|------|--------|----------|------|
| `go` | `went` | `gone` | 行く |
| `eat` | `ate` | `eaten` | 食べる |
| `write` | `wrote` | `written` | 書く |
| `see` | `saw` | `seen` | 見る |
| `take` | `took` | `taken` | 取る |
| `speak` | `spoke` | `spoken` | 話す |
| `give` | `gave` | `given` | 与える |
| `know` | `knew` | `known` | 知る |
| `break` | `broke` | `broken` | 壊す |
| `begin` | `began` | `begun` | 始める |

## 解答

| 現在形 | 完了形 |
|--------|--------|
| I play games. | I have (played) games. |
| I go to school. | I have (gone) to school. |
| I eat breakfast. | I have (eaten) breakfast. |
| I write a letter. | I have (written) a letter. |
| I see the movie. | I have (seen) the movie. |
| I read the book. | I have (read) the book. |
| I take the bus. | I have (taken) the bus. |
| I make a cake. | I have (made) a cake. |
| I speak English. | I have (spoken) English. |
| I know the answer. | I have (known) the answer. |
