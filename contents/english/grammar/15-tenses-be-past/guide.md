# be動詞の過去形 - 解説

現在形:
I `am` happy.

## 過去形

I `was` happy.

- I / he / she / it → `was`
- you / we / they → `were`

## 活用パターン

| 時制 | 主語 | be動詞 | 例 |
|------|------|--------|------|
| 現在形 | I | `am` | I `am` happy. |
| 現在形 | he/she | `is` | He `is` happy. |
| 過去形 | I/he/she | `was` | I `was` happy. |
| 過去形 | you/we/they | `were` | You `were` happy. |
