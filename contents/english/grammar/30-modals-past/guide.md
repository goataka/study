# 助動詞の過去形 - 解説

現在形:
I `can` swim.

## 過去形

I `could` swim.

- 一部の助動詞には過去形がある
- 過去の能力・許可・推量を表す

## 過去形パターン

| 助動詞 | 過去形 | 例 |
|--------|--------|------|
| `can` | `could` | I `could` swim when I was five. |
| `will` | `would` | I `would` play games every day. |
| `may` | `might` | I `might` be wrong. |
| `must` | `had to` | I `had to` study hard. |
| `should` | `should have + 過去分詞` | I `should have studied` harder. |
