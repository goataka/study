# 目的格の関係代名詞 - 解説

2つの文:
I play a game. The game is fun.

## 目的格の関係代名詞

The game `which` I play is fun.

- 先行詞（人）→ `whom` / `who` / `that`
- 先行詞（物）→ `which` / `that`
- 目的格の関係代名詞は省略できる

## パターン

| 先行詞 | 関係代名詞 | 省略 | 例 |
|--------|-----------|------|------|
| 人 | `whom` / `who` / `that` | 可 | a boy (whom / who) I know |
| 物 | `which` / `that` | 可 | a game (which) I play |

## 解答

| 先行詞 | 文 |
|--------|------|
| 人 | I have a friend (whom / who) I met yesterday. |
| 物 | I have a book (which) I bought yesterday. |
| 物 | I have a pen (which / that) I use every day. |

## 英作文解答

| 日本語 | 英語 |
|--------|------|
| 私は昨日買った本を持っている。 | I have a book which I bought yesterday. |
| 私は毎日使うペンを持っている。 | I have a pen which I use every day. |

## 応用問題

2つの文を関係代名詞（whom / which / that）を使って1つにしましょう。

| 2つの文 | 1つの文 |
|---------|---------|
| She is the teacher. I respect her the most. | She is the teacher 　　　　　　　　　　　　 I respect the most. |
| This is the book. I told you about it. | This is the book 　　　　　　　　　　　　 I told you about. |
| He is the boy. I saw him at the station. | He is the boy 　　　　　　　　　　　　 I saw at the station. |
| That is the cake. My mother made it. | That is the cake 　　　　　　　　　　　　 my mother made. |
| She is the friend. I invited her to the party. | She is the friend 　　　　　　　　　　　　 I invited to the party. |

## 応用解答

| 2つの文 | 1つの文 |
|---------|---------|
| She is the teacher. I respect her the most. | She is the teacher `whom` I respect the most. |
| This is the book. I told you about it. | This is the book `which` I told you about. |
| He is the boy. I saw him at the station. | He is the boy `that` I saw at the station. |
| That is the cake. My mother made it. | That is the cake `that` my mother made. |
| She is the friend. I invited her to the party. | She is the friend `whom` I invited to the party. |
