# 関係副詞 - 解説

2つの文:
This is the place. I was born there.

## 関係副詞

This is the place `where` I was born.

- 場所 → `where`
- 時 → `when`
- 理由 → `why`

## パターン

| 意味 | 関係副詞 | 例 |
|------|---------|------|
| 場所 | `where` | the place `where` I was born |
| 時 | `when` | the day `when` I met you |
| 理由 | `why` | the reason `why` I came |
