# 否定形

肯定文:
I `can play` games.

## 📚 否定形

I `cannot play` games.

- 助動詞の後に `not` をつける
- 短縮形: can't, won't, shouldn't, mustn't

## 📊 否定形パターン

| 助動詞 | 否定形 | 短縮形 | 例 |
|--------|--------|--------|------|
| `can` | `cannot` | `can't` | I `can't play` games. |
| `will` | `will not` | `won't` | I `won't play` games. |
| `must` | `must not` | `mustn't` | I `mustn't play` games. |
| `should` | `should not` | `shouldn't` | I `shouldn't play` games. |
| `may` | `may not` | — | I `may not play` games. |

- `must not` = ～してはいけない（禁止）
- `don't have to` = ～する必要はない（不必要）

## 📝 例

| 肯定文 | 否定文 |
|--------|--------|
| I can play games. | I (cannot / can't) play games. |
| I must go. | I (must not / mustn't) go. |
| I will come. | I (will not / won't) come. |
| I should stay. | I (should not / shouldn't) stay. |
| I may leave. | I (may not) leave. |
