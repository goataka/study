# 完了形の用法 - 解説

現在形:
I `play` games.

## 現在完了の3つの用法

### 経験

I `have played` this game before.（以前にこのゲームをしたことがある）

### 完了

I `have just finished` my homework.（ちょうど宿題を終えた）

### 継続

I `have lived` here for three years.（3年間ここに住んでいる）

## 用法パターン

| 用法 | キーワード | 例 |
|------|-----------|------|
| 経験 | before, ever, never | I `have played` this game `before`. |
| 完了 | just, already, yet | I `have just finished` my homework. |
| 継続 | for, since | I `have lived` here `for` three years. |

## 解答

| 文 | 用法 |
|------|------|
| I have been to Tokyo before. | 経験 |
| I have lived here for three years. | (継続) |
| I have just finished my homework. | (完了) |
| I have lost my key. | (結果) |
| I have never seen a lion. | (経験) |
| I have already eaten lunch. | (完了) |

## 英作文解答
