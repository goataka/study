# 時制の一致

直接話法:
He said, "I `play` games."

## 間接話法

He said that he `played` games.

- 主節が過去形のとき、従属節の時制も1つ過去にずれる

## 時制の一致パターン

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| 現在形 `play` | 過去形 `played` |
| 現在進行形 `am playing` | 過去進行形 `was playing` |
| 過去形 `played` | 過去完了 `had played` |
| 未来形 `will play` | `would play` |
| `can` | `could` |

## 例

| 直接話法 | 間接話法 |
|----------|----------|
| He said, "I play games." | He said that he (played) games. |
| He said, "I am playing games." | He said that he (was playing) games. |
| He said, "I played games." | He said that he (had played) games. |
| He said, "I will play games." | He said that he (would play) games. |
| He said, "I can play games." | He said that he (could play) games. |

## 英作文解答

| 日本語 | 英語 |
|--------|------|
| 彼はゲームをすると言った。 | He said that he played games. |
