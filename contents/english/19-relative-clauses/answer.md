# 関係代名詞ドリル - 解答

## 穴埋め問題

### 主格の関係代名詞

| 先行詞 | 文 |
|--------|------|
| 人 | I have a friend (who) speaks English. |
| 物 | I have a book (which) is very interesting. |
| 人・物 | I have a friend (that) lives in Tokyo. |

### 目的格の関係代名詞

| 先行詞 | 文 |
|--------|------|
| 人 | I have a friend (whom / who) I met yesterday. |
| 物 | I have a book (which) I bought yesterday. |
| 人・物 | I have a book (that) I like. |

### 所有格の関係代名詞

| 文 |
|------|
| I have a friend (whose) father is a doctor. |
| I have a book (whose) cover is red. |

### 関係副詞

| 意味 | 文 |
|------|------|
| 場所 | This is the place (where) I was born. |
| 時 | I remember the day (when) I met you. |
| 理由 | Tell me the reason (why) you were late. |

### 関係代名詞の省略

| 文 | 省略 |
|------|------|
| I have a friend who speaks English. | (×) 主格は省略不可 |
| I have a book which I bought. | (○) 目的格は省略可 |
| I have a friend that I met yesterday. | (○) 目的格は省略可 |

## 英作文

| 日本語 | 英語 |
|--------|------|
| 私には英語を話す友達がいる。 | I have a friend who speaks English. |
| 私は昨日買った本を持っている。 | I have a book which I bought yesterday. |
| お父さんが医者の友達がいる。 | I have a friend whose father is a doctor. |
| ここは私が生まれた場所です。 | This is the place where I was born. |
| 私が遅れた理由を教えます。 | I will tell you the reason why I was late. |
