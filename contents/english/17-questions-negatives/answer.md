# 疑問文・否定文ドリル - 解答

## 肯定文・否定文・疑問文の変化

肯定文を否定文と疑問文に変えましょう。

| 肯定文 | 否定文 | 疑問文 |
|--------|--------|--------|
| I play games. | I don't play games. | Do I play games? |
| You play games. | You don't play games. | Do you play games? |
| He plays games. | He doesn't play games. | Does he play games? |
| She plays games. | She doesn't play games. | Does she play games? |
| We play games. | We don't play games. | Do we play games? |
| They play games. | They don't play games. | Do they play games? |

## 過去形の疑問文と否定文

| 肯定文（過去形） | 否定文 | 疑問文 |
|------------------|--------|--------|
| I played games. | I didn't play games. | Did I play games? |
| He studied English. | He didn't study English. | Did he study English? |
| She watched TV. | She didn't watch TV. | Did she watch TV? |
| They went to school. | They didn't go to school. | Did they go to school? |
| We ate lunch. | We didn't eat lunch. | Did we eat lunch? |

| They are students. | They are not students. | Are they students? |
| I was tired. | I was not tired. | Was I tired? |

## 疑問詞を使った疑問文

下の文を日本語に訳しましょう。

| 英文 | 日本語訳 |
|------|----------|
| What do you do? | あなたは何をしますか。 |
| What did you do? | あなたは何をしましたか。 |
| When do you study? | あなたはいつ勉強しますか。 |
| When did you study? | あなたはいつ勉強しましたか。 |
| Where do you live? | あなたはどこに住んでいますか。 |
| Where did you go? | あなたはどこに行きましたか。 |
| Who is he? | 彼は誰ですか。 |
| Who did you meet? | あなたは誰に会いましたか。 |
| Why do you study English? | あなたはなぜ英語を勉強しますか。 |
| Why did you come here? | あなたはなぜここに来ましたか。 |
| How do you go to school? | あなたはどうやって学校に行きますか。 |
| How did you know that? | あなたはどうやってそれを知りましたか。 |
| Which do you like? | あなたはどちらが好きですか。 |
| Whose book is this? | これは誰の本ですか。 |

## How + 形容詞/副詞の疑問文

| 英文 | 日本語訳 |
|------|----------|
| How old are you? | あなたは何歳ですか。 |
| How tall is he? | 彼はどのくらいの身長ですか。 |
| How long have you lived here? | あなたはどのくらいここに住んでいますか。 |
| How far is it from here? | ここからどのくらい遠いですか。 |
| How often do you exercise? | あなたはどのくらいの頻度で運動しますか。 |
| How much is this? | これはいくらですか。 |
| How many books do you have? | あなたは何冊の本を持っていますか。 |

## 助動詞の疑問文と否定文

| I can swim. | I cannot swim. | Can I swim? |
| He will come. | He will not come. | Will he come? |
| She should study. | She should not study. | Should she study? |
| They must go. | They must not go. | Must they go? |
| You may leave. | You may not leave. | May I leave? |

## 付加疑問文

肯定文・否定文に付加疑問をつけましょう。

| 文 | 付加疑問文 |
|----|------------|
| You like music, | don't you? |
| He is a student, | isn't he? |
| She can swim, | can't she? |
| They will come, | won't they? |
| You don't like music, | do you? |
| He isn't a student, | is he? |
| She can't swim, | can she? |
| They won't come, | will they? |
