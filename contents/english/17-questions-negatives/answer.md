# 疑問文・否定文ドリル - 解答

## 穴埋め問題

### 一般動詞の疑問文

| 肯定文 | 疑問文 |
|--------|--------|
| I play games. | (Do) I play games? |
| I played games. | (Did) I play games? |
| I will play games. | (Will) I play games? |

### be動詞の疑問文

| 肯定文 | 疑問文 |
|--------|--------|
| I am happy. | (Am) I happy? |
| I was happy. | (Was) I happy? |
| I am playing games. | (Am) I playing games? |

### 一般動詞の否定文

| 肯定文 | 否定文 |
|--------|--------|
| I play games. | I (don't) play games. |
| I played games. | I (didn't) play games. |
| I will play games. | I (won't) play games. |

### be動詞の否定文

| 肯定文 | 否定文 |
|--------|--------|
| I am happy. | I (am not) happy. |
| I was happy. | I (was not / wasn't) happy. |
| I am playing games. | I (am not) playing games. |

### 疑問詞を使った疑問文

| 答え | 疑問文 |
|------|--------|
| I play games.（何を？） | (What) do I play? |
| I play games at home.（どこで？） | (Where) do I play games? |
| I play games every day.（いつ？） | (When) do I play games? |
| I play games because it's fun.（なぜ？） | (Why) do I play games? |

## 英作文

| 日本語 | 英語 |
|--------|------|
| 私はゲームをしますか？ | Do I play games? |
| 私はゲームをしません。 | I don't play games. |
| 私はゲームをしませんでした。 | I didn't play games. |
| 私は何をしますか？ | What do I do? |
| 私はなぜゲームをしますか？ | Why do I play games? |
| 私は幸せではありません。 | I am not happy. |
