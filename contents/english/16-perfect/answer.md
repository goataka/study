# 完了形ドリル - 解答

## 穴埋め問題

### have/hadの変化

| パターン | 文 |
|----------|------|
| 現在完了 | I have played games. |
| 過去完了 | I (had) played games. |
| 未来完了 | I (will have) played games. |

### 過去分詞の変化

| 現在形 | 完了形 |
|--------|--------|
| I play games. | I have (played) games. |
| I go to school. | I have (gone) to school. |
| I eat breakfast. | I have (eaten) breakfast. |
| I write a letter. | I have (written) a letter. |
| I see the movie. | I have (seen) the movie. |
| I read the book. | I have (read) the book. |
| I take the bus. | I have (taken) the bus. |
| I make a cake. | I have (made) a cake. |

### 完了形の用法

| 用法 | 文 |
|------|------|
| 経験 | I have (been) to Tokyo before. |
| 継続 | I have (lived) here for three years. |
| 完了 | I have just (finished) my homework. |
| 結果 | I have (lost) my key. |

## 英作文

| 日本語 | 英語 |
|--------|------|
| 私はゲームをしたことがある。 | I have played games. |
| 私はその映画を見たことがある。 | I have seen the movie. |
| 私は3年間ここに住んでいる。 | I have lived here for three years. |
| 私はちょうど宿題を終えた。 | I have just finished my homework. |
| 私は鍵をなくしてしまった。 | I have lost my key. |
