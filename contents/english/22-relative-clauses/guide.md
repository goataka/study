# 関係代名詞 - 解説

## 現在形との比較

現在形（2文）: I have a friend. He **speaks** English.
関係代名詞: I have a friend **who speaks** English.

- 2つの文を1つにまとめるのが関係代名詞

## 関係代名詞の種類

| 役割 | 人 | 物 | 人・物 |
|------|------|------|--------|
| 主格 | who | which | that |
| 目的格 | whom / who | which | that |
| 所有格 | whose | whose | — |

## 使い方

### 主格（～する人/物）

2文: I have a friend. He speaks English.
→ I have a friend **who** speaks English.

### 目的格（～した人/物）

2文: I have a book. I bought it yesterday.
→ I have a book **which** I bought yesterday.

### 所有格（～の）

2文: I have a friend. His father is a doctor.
→ I have a friend **whose** father is a doctor.

## 関係副詞

| 関係副詞 | 先行詞 | 例 |
|----------|--------|------|
| where | 場所 | the place **where** I was born |
| when | 時 | the day **when** I met you |
| why | 理由 | the reason **why** I was late |

## 省略のルール

- **目的格**の関係代名詞は省略できる（I have a book ~~which~~ I bought.）
- **主格**の関係代名詞は省略できない
