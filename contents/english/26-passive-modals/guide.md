# 助動詞の受動態 - 解説

能動態:
I `can play` the piano.

受動態:
The piano `can be played` by me.

- `助動詞` + `be` + 過去分詞

## 助動詞の受動態

| 能動態 | 受動態 |
|--------|--------|
| I `can play` | The piano `can be played` |
| I `must finish` | The work `must be finished` |
| I `should clean` | The room `should be cleaned` |
