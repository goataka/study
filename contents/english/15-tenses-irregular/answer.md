# 不規則動詞の過去形ドリル - 解答

## 穴埋め問題

| 現在形 | 過去形 |
|--------|--------|
| I go to school. | I (went) to school. |
| I eat breakfast. | I (ate) breakfast. |
| I write a letter. | I (wrote) a letter. |
| I run fast. | I (ran) fast. |
| I have a dog. | I (had) a dog. |
| I read books. | I (read) books. |
| I see a bird. | I (saw) a bird. |
| I take the bus. | I (took) the bus. |
| I come home early. | I (came) home early. |
| I make a cake. | I (made) a cake. |
| I get up at seven. | I (got) up at seven. |
| I know the answer. | I (knew) the answer. |
| I speak English. | I (spoke) English. |
| I think so. | I (thought) so. |
| I buy a book. | I (bought) a book. |

## 英作文

| 日本語 | 英語 |
|--------|------|
| 私は学校に行きました。 | I went to school. |
| 私は朝食を食べました。 | I ate breakfast. |
| 私は手紙を書きました。 | I wrote a letter. |
| 私は本を買いました。 | I bought a book. |
